The Story
Behind the Shell
Chapter One · Ecology
Made from nothing wasted
Every year, Vietnam's food industry discards billions of eggshells — calcium-rich, structurally complex, and entirely overlooked. Kippy Coop began with a single question: what if this material could become something extraordinary?

We collect shells from local producers in Quảng Nam province, clean them through a waterless process, and prepare them for the hands of our artisans. Nothing is shipped far. Nothing is synthesised. The shell is the material — unchanged in essence, transformed in form.
Chapter Two · Craft
40 hours per lamp
Our artisans in Hội An's old town spend an average of 40 hours on a single lamp shade. Each fragment is hand-selected for tone and texture, applied piece by piece in a mosaic tradition that draws from centuries of Vietnamese lacquerware.

No two pieces are identical. The irregularities are not imperfections — they are the signature of human hands, present in every surface. We do not sand them away.
Chapter Three · Heritage
Hội An and the lantern tradition
Hội An has been a city of light for over four centuries. Its famous lanterns — hung across every alley and canal — represent a living tradition of artisanship that survived colonisation, war, and globalisation.

Kippy Coop is rooted in this heritage. Our forms echo the organic geometry of the traditional lồng đèn, reimagined through a contemporary lens. We work with families who have made lanterns for generations, extending their practice rather than replacing it.
Chapter Four · Material
Eggshell paper — a new surface
Beyond lighting, we have developed a proprietary eggshell paper — a tactile, off-white sheet embedded with crushed shell particles that catch light in unexpected ways. It is used for wrapping, stationery, and as an art medium in its own right.

The paper is made in small runs using recycled mulberry pulp from Mekong Delta producers. Each sheet is slightly different in weight and texture — qualities that make it beloved by artists and designers who work with it.
